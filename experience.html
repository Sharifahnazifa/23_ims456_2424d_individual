My Experience

"KEJOHANAN BADMINTON TERBUKA UITM KOTA BHARU 2023"

One of my best experiences as a student at UITM CAWANGAN KELANTAN KAMPUS KOTA BHARU was participating as an Exco Protokol for 'kejohanan Badminton Terbuka UiTM Kota Bharu' that was held on June 17, 2023. The venue of the tournament was Dewan Badminton Tengku Anis, Pengakalan Chepa, Kota Bharu. This thrilling tournament was called "Smashin' Showdown," as badminton enthusiasts from all over Kelantan gathered to showcase their skills and passion for the sport. Participating as an Exco Protokol for this badminton tournament can be an exhilarating and rewarding experience for me as a badminton enthusiast. Besides, badminton tournaments provide an excellent opportunity to meet new people who share their passion for the sport. Engaging with fellow players, congratulating players after a match, and exchanging friendly banter can create a sense of camaraderie. I feel very happy to take part in this tournament.
